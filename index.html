Steak Frites with Chimicurri

A universal, versatile steak dish with parmesan truffle frites and an oil based chimicurri.

  • Steak, cut and temp of your choice
  • Golden potatoes cut in thinly shoestring style shape.
  • Truffle oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Cilantro
  • Parsley
  • Red or white wine vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Chili powder
  • Onions

After cooking the steak to your preferred temperature, blend the oil, vinegar, cilantro, onions, and seasoning to make the chimchurri sauce. Fry potatoes until golden brown. Lighty salt , plate, and enjoy!

Grilled Cheese

The original sandwich everyone loves.

  • Bread of your choice. My choice will be Sourdough
  • The cheese of your choice. My choice will be provolone and monterey jack.
  • Butter

Stack your desired amount of cheese between two slices of bread. Place butter in a pan on medium heat. Season between bread if desire, toast both sides, plate and enjoy!

Quick Blueberry Pancakes

Of course they don't have to be blueberry, but why not right?

  • Just add water pancake mix
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Blueberries
  • Milk

Crack two eggs into a bowl of pancake mix and add milk to mix smoothly. After mixing thoroughly, add blueberries and heat pan on medium heat. Pour pancake mix into plan, wait 2-3 mins or when pancake starts to bubble. Flip, allow sides to cook evenly. Allow rest, apply butter and syrup. Enjoy.
